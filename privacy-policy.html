Privacy Policy

Trump Truth Social Alert — Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

Trump Truth Social Alert is a browser extension and email alert service that monitors President Trump's Truth Social account and delivers notifications. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Chrome Extension — Data Collection

The Chrome extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. It does not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or any third-party services.

Chrome Extension — Network Requests

The extension makes periodic requests to Truth Social's public API (truthsocial.com) and our own servers solely to check for new posts. No user data, identifiers, or authentication credentials are sent with these requests.

Chrome Extension — Local Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • The ID of the most recently seen post (to detect new ones)
  • Your notification preferences (enabled/disabled, polling interval)
  • A cache of recent posts for display in the popup

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

Chrome Extension — Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions needed to function: alarms (for periodic checking), notifications (to alert you), storage (to save preferences), and access to truthsocial.com (to fetch posts).

Email Alerts — Data Collection

If you subscribe to email alerts at trumptruthalerts.com, we collect your email address for the sole purpose of sending you notifications when President Trump posts on Truth Social. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party.

Email Alerts — How Your Data Is Used

  • Your email address is stored securely and used only to send Trump Truth Alert notifications.
  • Each alert email includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time and your address will be immediately deactivated.
  • To request permanent deletion of your data, reply to any alert email with the word "delete" and we will remove your address from our records.

Changes

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected here with an updated date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the extension's support page or reply to any alert email.
